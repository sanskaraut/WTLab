VIT Curriculum Highlights

Click the tabs below to learn about SDP, EDI, DT, and Course Projects.

Skill Development Program (SDP)

SDP focuses on enhancing technical skills beyond the regular curriculum. It includes workshops, certifications, and hands-on training in emerging technologies like AI, Cloud Computing, and Blockchain.

  • Practical skill acquisition
  • Industry-ready certifications
  • Hands-on workshops

Engineering Design and Innovation (EDI)

EDI is a unique component that encourages students to apply engineering principles to solve real-world problems. It fosters a culture of innovation and design thinking from the first year.

  • Project-based learning
  • Interdisciplinary collaboration
  • Prototyping and testing

Design Thinking (DT)

Design Thinking teaches a human-centric approach to problem-solving. Students learn to empathize with users, define problems, ideate solutions, and create prototypes.

  • Empathy mapping
  • Ideation techniques
  • User-centric solutions

Course Projects

Every core subject at VIT includes a course project. This ensures that students don't just learn theory but also implement concepts to build working models or software.

  • Subject-specific application
  • Teamwork and coordination
  • Continuous evaluation
